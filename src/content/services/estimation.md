## Precise Cost Projections

Accurate estimation is crucial for project success. We provide detailed cost estimates to help you plan your finances effectively.

### Services Offered:
*   **Preliminary Estimates:** Initial cost approximations for feasibility.
*   **Detailed Estimation:** Comprehensive breakdown of material and labor costs.
*   **Bill of Quantities (BOQ):** Preparation of detailed BOQ for tendering.
*   **Rate Analysis:** Analysis of current market rates for materials and labor.
