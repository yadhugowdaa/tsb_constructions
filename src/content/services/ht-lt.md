### Transmission & Distribution Excellence

We are certified experts in High Tension (HT) and Low Tension (LT) line installations. From setting up electric poles to commissioning transformers, we handle end-to-end electrical infrastructure.

**Our Expertise:**
* **Transformer Installation:** Commissioning of distribution transformers.
* **Overhead Lines:** Erecting poles and stringing conductors safely.
* **Liaisoning:** We handle all approvals and paperwork with BESCOM/KPTCL.

Safety and compliance are our top priorities in every electrical project we undertake.
